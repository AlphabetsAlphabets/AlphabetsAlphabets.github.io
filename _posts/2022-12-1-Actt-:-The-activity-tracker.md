# Why did I make this?
The reason is quite simple. I think of myself as someone productive, someone who uses his own time well and spends on it doing something useful. I do everything that is scheduled the day before on my calender. I also think of myself that wastes no time, and gets the job done. The keyword being *think*, I *think* of myself as someone with all those qualities. But, do I possess those qualites? Well the application is going to answer only the first question.

# What exactly does it do?
1. Well, like any other activity tracker it trackes your activities. It's going to be manual as opposed to automatic. You simply enter the name of the activity, a tag or category of the activity. Click on "start" and begin. It keeps track of how long you worked on that thing for. 
2. Once you're done click "stop" and you'll be shown a history of what you've done alongside its name, tag and how long you spent doing that thing.
3. You can take a look at a "Sunburst" a sunburst is essentially just a super fancy pie chart with a hole in the center, so a donut. The sunburst will let you see how long you spend on activites under the same tag. This will grant you an objective view of what you spend your time doing. This will fulfill the main objective of Actt, getting an objective look at how you spend your time.
