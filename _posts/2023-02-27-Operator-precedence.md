Operator precedence is used in many, many applications. Such as calculators, programming languages and many more. What is operator precedence? Basically it determines the order of operations. Just like how you always deal with the multiplication first, then addition. Or exponents first then multiplication. Implementing this took me a while. My exercise for learning how to build operator precedence is by making a calculator called "[Kalculator](https://github.com/AlphabetsAlphabets/Kalculator)" which is very incomplete, and I got very bored of it and decided to move onto something else.
